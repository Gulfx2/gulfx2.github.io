Bitterness is a paralytic，love is a much more vicious motivator.

痛苦是种麻醉剂，爱则是更危险的动机

I love three things in this world. Sun, moon and you. Sun for morning, moon for night, and you forever.

浮世万千，吾爱有三。日月与卿，日起为昼，月升为暮，卿为朝暮。

All lives end, all hearts are bro.

生命终会终结，人心都会破碎。

Once you’re ruled out the impossible. Whatever remains, however improbable, must be true.

一旦你排除了所有的不可能，还剩什么，不管多么难以置信，一定就是真相。
